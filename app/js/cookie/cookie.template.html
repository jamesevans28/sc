Where do I find it?

You need to be on your desktop. Browse to the supercoach site, log in, and look at your cookies. In chrome this is the step by step:
  1. Press F12 to bring up the dev tools
  2. Go to the application tab
  3. Expand the Cookies menu, and select http://supercoach.heraldsun.com.au
  4. Find the record that has a name of PHPSESSID
  5. Put the value into the box above and save. This number will be 32 characters long.
